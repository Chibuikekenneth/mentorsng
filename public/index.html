How It Works

No worries at all, we help you reach out the people you need. We know the power of mentoring and we are here to help.

Sign up

Register with us and complete your profile. We will suggest mentors for you immediately if there is match. If not, we will notify you when you have a match.

Goals and Timelines

Accept the terms of mentoring and off you are to start.

Collaboration

You get to connect and collaborate with people in your dream field

Monitoring

We wont just leave you to yourself, we will continue to monitor the progress of your mentoring.

Time Based Mentoring

The Mentoring Season is 10-months and teams are expected to achieve all their set goals for the mentoring season.

Messaging

You can always communicate with your mentors based on mutual agreement.
